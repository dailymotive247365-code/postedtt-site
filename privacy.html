Privacy Policy

Posted TT — Effective Date: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Posted TT respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our desktop application.

2. Information We Collect

Posted TT collects only the minimum information necessary to function: your TikTok OAuth access token (stored locally on your device) and basic TikTok account information (username, display name) retrieved during authentication.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to authenticate with TikTok and publish video content on your behalf. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your computer. Posted TT does not transmit your data to any servers other than TikTok's official API endpoints required for publishing content.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only sent to TikTok as required to publish your content.

6. Data Retention

Your OAuth tokens are stored locally until you revoke access or uninstall the application. You can delete your stored credentials at any time by removing the local configuration files.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including storing credentials locally and using secure HTTPS connections for all API communications.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information.
